Acidity

Reasons :

  1. Dietary Choices:

    • Consuming spicy, fatty, or fried foods.
    • Excessive intake of caffeine, alcohol, or carbonated beverages.
    • Overeating or eating large meals.

  2. Lifestyle Factors:

    • Smoking
    • Lack of physical activity.
    • Eating close to bedtime.

  3. Medical Conditions:

    • Obesity
    • Pregnancy

  4. Medications:

    • Certain painkillers (e.g., NSAIDs)
    • Antidepressants
    • Blood pressure medications

  5. Stress and Anxiety:

    • Stress and anxiety can increase stomach acid production and delay stomach emptying.

Ayurvedic Remedies :

  1. Amla (Indian Gooseberry):

    2. Amla is rich in vitamin C and helps in reducing acid levels in the stomach. Consuming fresh amla juice or powder with water can be beneficial.

  2. Licorice (Yashtimadhu):

    3. Licorice root helps soothe the stomach lining and reduce acid secretion. Chewing licorice root or consuming it in the form of tea can provide relief.

  3. Cumin Seeds (Jeera):

    4. Cumin seeds aid in digestion and reduce acidity. Boil cumin seeds in water and drink the decoction after meals.

  4. Ginger (Adrak):

    5. Ginger aids in digestion and reduces stomach acidity. Consuming ginger tea or fresh ginger with honey can provide relief.

  5. Aloe Vera Gel:

    6. Aloe vera juice helps soothe the stomach lining and reduce acid production. Drinking a small amount of aloe vera juice before meals can be effective.

  6. Basil Leaves (Tulsi):

    7. Basil leaves have carminative properties and help in reducing acidity. Chewing basil leaves or drinking basil tea can be beneficial.

  7. Coriander Seeds (Dhania):

    8. Coriander seeds help in digestion and reduce acidity. Boil coriander seeds in water and drink the strained water.
