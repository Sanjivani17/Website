Headache

Reasons :

  1. Tension Headaches:

    • Caused by stress, anxiety, poor posture, or muscle tension in the neck and shoulders.

  2. Migraine:

    • Severe, throbbing pain often accompanied by nausea, vomiting, and sensitivity to light and sound. Triggers can include certain foods, hormonal changes, stress, and sensory stimuli.

  3. Cluster Headaches:

    • Intense, recurring headaches that occur in clusters, often around one eye. Triggers can include alcohol, smoking, and changes in sleep patterns.

  4. Sinus Headaches:

    • Result from sinusitis or inflammation of the sinuses, leading to pain around the forehead, cheeks, and eyes.

  5. Dehydration:

    • Lack of adequate hydration can cause headaches.

  6. Eye Strain:

    • Prolonged use of digital screens or incorrect eyeglass prescriptions can cause headaches.

Ayurvedic Remedies :

  1. Ginger (Adrak):

    2. Ginger has anti-inflammatory and analgesic properties. Drinking ginger tea or applying ginger paste to the forehead can provide relief.

  2. Peppermint Oil:

    3. Peppermint oil has a cooling effect and can help reduce headache pain. Massaging the temples with diluted peppermint oil can be beneficial.

  3. Eucalyptus Oil:

    4. Eucalyptus oil can help relieve sinus headaches. Inhaling steam with a few drops of eucalyptus oil can provide relief.

  4. Cinnamon (Dalchini):

    5. Cinnamon has anti-inflammatory properties. Making a paste of cinnamon and water and applying it to the forehead can provide relief.

  5. Lavender Oil:

    6. Lavender oil has calming and relaxing properties. Inhaling lavender oil or applying it to the temples can help reduce headache pain.
