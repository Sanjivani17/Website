HeatDamage

Heat damage can also cause the pigments of your hair, whether it's natural or dyed, to oxidize. The result is faded coloring, especially when hair has high porosity. If you dye your hair, this might mean that you need to apply color more often, which can cause further damage.

Ayurvedic Remedies :

  1. Coconut oil :

    2. Coconut oil is an effective natural remedy for heat-damaged hair. It is rich in fatty acids that penetrate the hair shaft, providing deep hydration and preventing further damage. You can warm some coconut oil and apply it to your hair.

  2. apple cider vinegar:

    3. Hart suggests combining a quarter cup of apple cider vinegar with two cups of water, then misting it onto damp hair after shampooing and conditioning. This can be used as a weekly hair treatment.

  3. Aloe vera:

    4. Aloe vera is a powerful ingredient that has soothing and healing properties. It has anti-inflammatory properties that help reduce inflammation and promote hair growth. Simply mix fresh aloe vera gel with a few drops of your favourite essential oil and apply it to your hair and scalp.

  4. Honey:

    5. Calling honey “a great humectant,” a substance that aids in improving moisture, Scott-Lynch says that it can help draw moisture into the hair while making it shiny, too.
