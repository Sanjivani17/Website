Stomach Pain

Reasons :

  1. Indigestion (Dyspepsia):

    • Poor digestion due to overeating, eating too quickly, or consuming fatty or spicy foods.

  2. Gas and Bloating:

    • Excess gas in the digestive tract can cause discomfort and pain.

  3. Constipation:

    • Difficulty in passing stools can lead to abdominal pain.

  4. Gastritis:

    • Inflammation of the stomach lining, often caused by infections, alcohol, or certain medications.

  5. Food Intolerances:

    • Inability to digest certain foods, such as lactose or gluten, leading to stomach pain.

  6. Irritable Bowel Syndrome (IBS):

    • A chronic condition affecting the large intestine, causing cramping, abdominal pain, bloating, gas, and diarrhea or constipation.

Ayurvedic Remedies :

  1. Ginger (Adrak):

    2. Ginger has anti-inflammatory and digestive properties. Drinking ginger tea can help relieve stomach pain caused by indigestion and gas.

  2. Ajwain (Carom Seeds):

    3. Ajwain seeds help in digestion and relieve gas and bloating. Chewing a teaspoon of ajwain seeds with a pinch of salt can provide relief.

  3. Fennel Seeds (Saunf):

    4. Fennel seeds have carminative properties and can help relieve gas and bloating. Chewing fennel seeds or drinking fennel tea can be beneficial.

  4. Asafoetida (Hing):

    5. Hing has antispasmodic and carminative properties. Mixing a pinch of hing with warm water and consuming it can help relieve stomach pain.

  5. Aloe Vera Juice:

    6. Aloe vera juice has soothing properties and can help reduce inflammation in the digestive tract. Consuming aloe vera juice can provide relief from stomach pain.

  6. Peppermint (Pudina):

    7. Peppermint has antispasmodic properties and can help relax the digestive tract. Drinking peppermint tea can provide relief from stomach pain.

  7. Triphala:

    8. Triphala is a traditional Ayurvedic formulation that helps in digestion and detoxification. Taking Triphala powder with warm water before bedtime can help manage stomach pain.
